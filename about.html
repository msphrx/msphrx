ABOUT MY COMPANY

Learn more about Peter

I started as a print designer, and gravitated toward the web in 2000. I learned all I could about Content Management Systems. I found that MODX gives me the creative freedom to say "Yes" to my clients.

When you become my client you're getting the whole nine yards of my experience in various marketing shops. Marketing is good because that's where one learns how money is actually made.

I've found many e-commerce `solutions` to be frustrating and stressful. Around that time Brett and Luke at FoxyCart did something about it. A few years later David at FoxyTools took the data feed from FoxyCart to create OrderDesk. Two of my clients can't live without it. Both are awesome solutions for small businesses.

I specialize in giving your business what it needs, along with solid marketing advice, strategies and tactics to consider implementing, and occasionally some print work.
