eCommerce 101

Orbiting since: Mar 07, 2013 by Stratos  | Tags: ecommerce, order management, dwolla, square  | Comments (0)

Tail wagging the dog and stealing all the food? Then use FoxyCart and OrderDesk regardless of your CMS!

eCommerce has come a very long ways in the past 15 years. My preference for solution is determined by a few common sense factors:

  1. Does it integrate easily with the website?
  2. Does it have ordering management tools?
  3. Will it work with a wide variety of merchant accounts?

First off, read a primer on the basics of eCommerce. This gives you a good idea of what's involved.

1: Easily Integrates?

eCommerce is fraught with so many possibilities that it's overwhelming and very easy to wind up with a tail wagging your dog that will cost you more money than you'll be comfortable with.

FoxyCart is my choice because it is flexible and separate from your website. In other words, FoxyCart does it's job very well and can be popped into any page anywhere on your site. Simple. Easy. Done.

2: Order Management

OrderDesk is a really amazing piece of web-based software. So simple and concise that you will not believe it.

The above shows an actual OrderDesk overview. FOr details just click anywhere on the order to reveal details:

3: Merchant Accounts
Beware the Jabberwocky. I didn't get that either, but merchant accounts fall into murky territory. Don't fall for the brick and mortar solutions. Payment gateway/merchant accounts are the fast-changing areas of eCommerce. Do yourself a favor: check out Dwolla and Square for both desktop and mobile solutions.

This is just a small slice of the wide scope of FoxyCart and OrderDesk. Both are used by a wide variety of businesses worldwide. Both allow you to turn them off as needed if your business is seasonal such as honey bees or a farmers market stand.
